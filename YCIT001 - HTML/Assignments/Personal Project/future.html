MULTISENSES POETRY BOOK

Sensorily pleasing expanding the reading to an hearing. I am a fan poetry but as much I love reading, my head goes everywhere. The idea for this project is creating a visual support while the poems are read. This whole piece would be accompanied of a unique composition made and chose for each of the piece. Rediscovering poetry by transmetting it into another form. Creating an album, a visual album of poetry.
